Most businesses run their back office and their front office on two different islands. Finance lives in one system, sales lives in another, and reconciling them is somebody's full-time job. Corebalt **ERP** and **CRM** are built to remove that gap — they share one secure core, so operations and growth see the same data.

You can explore both at **[erp.corebalt.co.ke](https://erp.corebalt.co.ke)**.

## Corebalt ERP — run the whole back office

ERP is where the business actually operates:

- **Finance & accounting** — ledgers, invoicing, payments and reconciliation
- **HR & payroll** — from onboarding to payslips, with statutory deductions handled
- **Procurement & inventory** — purchase orders, suppliers and stock in one place
- **Reporting** — real-time dashboards that give leadership a single source of truth

No more month-end archaeology across disconnected tools.

## Corebalt CRM — capture and close

CRM is where revenue is won:

- **Lead capture** from every channel, with nothing falling through the cracks
- **Pipelines** that show exactly where each deal stands
- **Automated follow-ups** over SMS and WhatsApp so no lead goes cold
- **Analytics** on conversion, sources and team performance

## Why "one connected core" matters

Because ERP and CRM share identity and data, a closed deal in CRM flows into invoicing in ERP without re-keying. A customer's payment history, support history and order history are one record, not three. Leadership gets one dashboard, not a reconciliation headache.
