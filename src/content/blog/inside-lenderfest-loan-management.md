Lending is unforgiving software to get wrong. Money goes out, money must come back, and every step in between is regulated, audited and counted. **[LenderFest](https://www.lenderfest.loans)** is our flagship platform built for exactly that — a complete loan management system (LMS) for digital lenders, SACCOs and microfinance institutions.

Here's how it runs the full lifecycle.

## Origination and scoring

A loan starts as an application. LenderFest captures it through web, mobile or agent channels, runs your eligibility rules, and scores the borrower against the signals you trust — repayment history, bureau data, alternative data, or your own custom model. Decisions are consistent, explainable and logged.

## Disbursement and repayments

Approved loans disburse straight to mobile money or bank, and repayments come back the same way. M-Pesa is native — STK push for collections, automated reconciliation, and instant receipts — alongside cards and bank rails for markets beyond Kenya. Every cent is matched to a loan automatically.

## Collections that actually collect

Arrears are where lenders win or lose. LenderFest tracks aging buckets, automates reminders over SMS and WhatsApp, schedules follow-ups, and gives collections teams a prioritized worklist instead of a spreadsheet. Restructures, write-offs and provisioning are all first-class.

## Reporting that keeps you licensed

Regulators want numbers in a specific shape. LenderFest produces CBK-aligned portfolio, provisioning and arrears reporting out of the box — and the same engine extends to other jurisdictions as you expand.
