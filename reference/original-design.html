Data & Analytics · Big Data

Turn raw data into decisions. We build pipelines, warehouses and dashboards — and handle big-data and streaming workloads — so leaders see what's happening live and can forecast what's next.

Modern stack: Apache Spark & Hadoop for big data, Kafka for real-time streaming, Snowflake, BigQuery & Databricks for warehousing, dbt for transformation, Airflow for orchestration, and Power BI, Tableau or Looker for visualization — with Python and ML for forecasting.

  • Spark / Hadoop
  • Snowflake / BigQuery
  • Kafka streaming
  • Power BI / Tableau
  • ML & forecasting
Why Corebalt

Engineered for how Kenya does business.

Local payments, local compliance, local support — built in from day one, not bolted on later.

M-Pesa & Daraja

STK push, C2B, B2C and reconciliation are native across every product — money moves the way your customers expect.

KRA & CBK compliance

eTIMS e-invoicing, VAT and lending reporting aligned to Kenyan regulation, so audits and licensing stay straightforward.

People you can reach

A Nairobi-based team that answers the phone — onboarding, training and support in your timezone, in your language.

One connected core

Secure by design
